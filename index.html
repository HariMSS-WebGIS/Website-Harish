My Journey

I am a passionate Web-GIS and Web Developer based in Hyderabad. My professional career bridges the gap between conventional software development and complex geographic information systems. I specialize in designing responsive geospatial web portals, automating data processing workflows, and integrating massive spatial datasets.

Over my career, I've had the privilege of working with leading national organizations, including INCOIS (Indian National Centre for Ocean Information Services) and NRSC-ISRO (National Remote Sensing Centre), contributing to portals like Bhuvan Panchayat and weather telemetry applications.
